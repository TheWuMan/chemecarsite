Recruitment
Interested in Joining ChemE Car?

ChemE Car is currently recruiting!


The ChemE Car project team is in the midst of recruiting season! We build cars that are powered and stopped via chemical reactions and we compete in the American Institute of Chemical Engineers' official ChemE Car competition. The work we do challenges us to explore different alternative energy sources in the face of an uncertain energy future. Our team has a history of success at both the Regional and National level, with multiple victories at both competitions.

Although we are affiliated with chemical engineering, our team has members from all backgrounds, including electrical, mechanical, biological engineering, as well as computer science. ChemE Car is an excellent opportunity to practically apply what you learn in your engineering classes. Aside from the technical experience, you can learn a lot about working in teams and you'll make lasting friendships with people both in and outside your major!

We are recruiting for the Fall 2016 semester with upcoming Information sessions in the spring, where you will have the opportunity to meet our team leaders as well as learn more about our sub-teams and the application process. More information to come. If you have any questions feel free to email us.
